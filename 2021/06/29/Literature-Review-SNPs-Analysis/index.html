Literature Review: SNPs Analysis

Author: John Doe Date: June 29, 2021  22:34:24

paper origin: Oxidative stress response and nitrogen utilization are strongly variable in Saccharomyces cerevisiae wine strains with different fermentation performances

Method

Dataset Origin:

  • Vineyard strains P283, P301, R008, R103.

  • Leibniz Institute DSMZ-German
    Collection of Microorganisms and Cell Cultures
    (Braunschweig, Germany) DSM 28395, DSM 28396, DSM 28397 and DSM 28398.

  • CBS collection (CBS8803) S288c.

  • The industrialwine strain Lalvin EC1118

Experiment Procedure

  1. Fermentation in bioreactors
    1-l Multifors bioreactors

  2. Get the FAN, $NH_3$, $NH_4^+$ concentration

  3. Do HPLC analysis.

  4. RNA-seq analysis using the SOLiD sequence

  • Use RiboPure Yeast kit to extract total RNA
  • Measure the quality and quantity of the total RNA by Nanodrop and the Agilent 2100 bioanalyzer.
  • Remove rRNAs from total RNA by RiboMinus Transcriptome Isolation Kit
  • Submit RNA-seq data to GEO database with accession numbers GSE44845, GSM1092504-GSM1092515.
  • Outline the positions of reads in SAM format.
  • Use DEGseq software to identify signigicantly differnetially expressed gene use MARS model.
  • filter gene whose p value is larger than 1e-4.
  • Use MeV software to analyze Cluster.
  • classify all genes by Go Slim classes. Compute the distances among each Go Slim class.
  • Analyze SNPs by snpEff software.
